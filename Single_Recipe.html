Beyti Kebab

Estimated time for preparing - 25min.

Discription of the dish

This dish is a delightful presentation of crispy rolled wraps, generously filled with a savory mixture and grilled to perfection. Topped with a drizzle of tangy tomato sauce and a sprinkle of black sesame seeds, it boasts a visually appealing golden-brown finish. At the center, a creamy yogurt-based dip, enhanced with aromatic spices and fresh parsley, adds a refreshing and zesty balance to the rich flavors of the rolls. Freshly sliced tomatoes and roasted green peppers surround the dish, providing a burst of color and a complementary freshness to every bite. Perfect as a main course or appetizer, this dish is a harmonious blend of textures and flavors.

Instruction

  1. Layer the vegetables in a large pot with the potatoes on the bottom. Fill with water and add salt.
  2. Bring to a boil and cook until vegetables are tender.
  3. Meanwhile, sauté the chilé powder, turmeric, cumin, coriander, and cayenne in the cooking oil and then add to the soup.
  4. Add garlic at the end before serving.
